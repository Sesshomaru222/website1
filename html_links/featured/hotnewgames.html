Hot New Games of 2025

With 2025 coming to an end, I think it's a great time to look back at the hottest games this year has bestowed upon us. Join me as I argue about what I believe to be some of the greatest games of this year!

1. Starfield

Starfield is Bethesda's first new IP in over 25 years, and it certainly lives up to the hype. With its vast open-world space exploration, deep RPG mechanics, and stunning visuals, Starfield offers an unparalleled gaming experience. The game's rich lore and engaging storyline make it a must-play for any sci-fi enthusiast.

2. The Legend of Zelda: Echoes of the Past

This latest installment in the Zelda franchise takes players on a nostalgic journey while introducing innovative gameplay mechanics. The beautifully crafted world, intricate puzzles, and captivating story make Echoes of the Past a standout title in 2025.

3. Cyberpunk 2077: Neon Uprising

After a rocky start, Cyberpunk 2077 has redeemed itself with the Neon Uprising expansion. The new content adds depth to the game's narrative and introduces exciting gameplay features that enhance the overall experience. The vibrant cityscape and dynamic characters make it a thrilling adventure.

4. Horizon Forbidden West: Lost Horizons

This expansion to Horizon Forbidden West expands on the already impressive world of Aloy. With new regions to explore, formidable machines to battle, and a gripping storyline, Lost Horizons solidifies its place as one of the top games of 2025.

5. Resident Evil Village: Shadows of the Past

The latest entry in the Resident Evil series delivers a chilling experience with its Shadows of the Past expansion. The eerie atmosphere, intense gameplay, and compelling narrative make it a standout horror game this year.
