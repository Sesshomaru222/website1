Hottest Anime of 2025

This is my personally curated list of what I have found to be the hottest, most anticipated anime of 2025. These picks are just my opinions and they are not in any particular order.

Kaiju No.8

I haven't been this excited about a new anime in a long timeâ€”since the early toonami era. Kaiju number 8 has an exciting plot that will keep your interest for seasons to come. The character developement is top-notch, the animation quality is exceptional, and the soundtrack is phenomenal. If you love action, adventure, and a good story, then Kaiju No. 8 is the anime for you. Don't miss out on what is sure to be one of the best anime of 2025!

Shangri-la Frontier

I have to admit, this anime took me by surprise. Shangri-la Frontier is a refreshing take on the isekai genre, with a unique setting and a protagonist who is both relatable and likable. The anime is quarky and fun, with plenty of action and humor to keep you entertained. The animation is crisp, the story is engaging, and the characters are lovable. If you're looking for a new anime that will make you laugh and keep you on the edge of your seat, then Shangri-la Frontier just might be the perfect choice.

Dr Stone S3

Dr. Stone has been one of my favorite anime since it first aired, and I'm thrilled to see it returning for a third season. The series continues to impress with its unique blend of science, adventure, and humor. The characters are well-developed, and the story is both engaging and educational. Season 3 promises to bring even more exciting adventures as Senku and his friends continue their quest to rebuild civilization using the power of science. If you haven't watched Dr. Stone yet, now is the perfect time to catch up before the new season premieres!
