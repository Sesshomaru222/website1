Hottest Anime of 2025

This is my personally curated list of what I have found to be the hottest, most anticipated anime of 2025. These picks are just my opinions and they are not in any particular order.

Kaiju No.8

I haven't been this excited about a new anime in a long timeâ€”since the early toonami era. Kaiju number 8 has an exciting plot that will keep your interest for seasons to come. The character developement is top-notch, the animation quality is exceptional, and the soundtrack is phenomenal. One of the standouts about this anime is the unbelievable quality of the intro. As a 3D modeler myself, I find the exceptionally high quality, intricate details, and chaotic beauty of the intro to be absolutely stunning. Overall, if you love action, adventure, and a good story, then Kaiju No. 8 is the anime for you. Don't miss out on what is sure to be one of the best anime of 2025!

Shangri-la Frontier

I have to admit, this anime took me by surprise. When I first put watched it on Crunchyroll, I didn't expect much. I figured I would just kill some time until an anime I was actually interested in began producing content. I was pleasently surprised by how this anime grabbed my attention. Now I'm hooked! Shangri-la Frontier is a refreshing take on the isekai genre, with a unique setting and a protagonist who is both relatable and likable. The anime is quarky and fun, with plenty of action and humor to keep you entertained. The animation is crisp, the story is engaging, and the characters are lovable. If you're looking for a new anime that will make you laugh and keep you on the edge of your seat, then Shangri-la Frontier just might be the perfect choice.

Dr Stone S3

Dr. Stone has been one of my favorite anime since it first aired, and I'm thrilled to see it returning for a third season. The series continues to impress with its unique blend of science, adventure, and humor. The characters are well-developed, and the story is both engaging and educational. Season 3 promises to bring even more exciting adventures as Senku and his friends continue their quest to rebuild civilization using the power of science. If you haven't watched Dr. Stone yet, now is the perfect time to catch up before the new season premieres!

Dragonball Daima

Dragonball has long been one of my top anime, and the foundation of my anime journey. Akira Toriyama's passing was a very sad day for fans across the world, it was a deeply felt loss. Daima serves as a memorial to his legacy, and a celebration of the world he created.

The quick version: Daima is an action-packed adventure that follows Goku and his friends as they face off against a new threat. Goku and a handful of the gang step foot into the demon world to defeate bad guys and save the day, etc etc. The things that stands out about Daima are two-fold—quality: the animation quality is some of the best I've seen. The fight scenes are incredible even for newer dragonball standards. nostalgia: Daima has a familiar feel to it. It feels like a wonderful blend of GT, Dragonball, and Super. It's adventurous, it's dynamic, and it's historically rich. That's right, Daima adds tons of lore to the universe! It expands on the idea of gods and demons in a very Dragonball way. If you care about the lore then you'll want to pay close attention as the mystery of the dragonballs deepens, the origin of the kais and Namekians are explored, and the environment that led to Buu's creationm are developed further.
